Language:
Language used in TaskUnifier. You have to restart TaskUnifier in order to apply to selected language.
Date format:
Date format used to display dates.
Time format:
Time format used to display hours.
Scheduler delay:
Every n seconds TaskUnifier will synchronize your tasks.
You can enable/disable the scheduler by clicking on the corresponding icon in the toolbar.
Show completed tasks:
Show/hide the completed tasks in the contexts, folders, goals and locations searcher lists.
Show completed tasks at the end:
Show the completed tasks at the end in the general, contexts, folders, goals and locations searcher lists.
